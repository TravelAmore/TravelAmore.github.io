Upcoming Tour Oct. 8th-22th

Veneto

Begin your journey with an in-depth cultural tour of Northern Italy. From enchanting Venice to Lake Como, Northern Italy offers an abundance of natural beauty and historic charm. Spend 4 days enjoying many of the region’s highlights, starting with leisure time to explore the iconic canals and ornate architecture of Venice. Travel to Verona to experience the medieval magic that inspired Shakespeare’s ‘Romeo and Juliet. Through your guided walking tour, experience Verona's well-preserved monuments from antiquity, including a 1st-century Roman amphitheater still in use today. Visit picturesque waterfront towns along Lake Garda and Lake Como, nestled in the foothills of the Italian Alps. Enjoy dinner in Milan and a morning sightseeing tour of the city’s historic highlights and modern glamour.

Tuscany

Tuscany is a dream come true. Immerse yourself in the life of an enchanting region and experience beautiful Tuscany ... more like a Tuscan than a tourist. Over 4 incredible days, you'll have a chance to go wine tasting in Chianti, visit medieval castles, see art treasures of the Renaissance and explore Tuscany's picturesque towns and villages. Visit the five small, medieval villages know as Cinque Terre which is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Your visit includes the lovely Cortona, made famous by Frances Mayes book "Under the Tuscan Sun". Cortona’s architecture is medieval with steep narrow streets situated on a hillside, embracing a view of the whole of the Valdichiana. You will travel through the enchanting Tuscan countryside to the Gothic and Romanesque town of Siena, considered one of the world’s best-preserved medieval cities and a showcase of Italian Gothic. I will take you on a guided tour of the city’s highlights. End your trip with breakfast at a sidewalk cafe, soaking in the splendid views of Montecatini.

Campania

Spend 3 days marveling in the beauty of the Amalfi Coast, often referred to as "the most beautiful coastline of Italy". With it's amazing mountains and spectacular sea views, the Amalfi Coast captures everyone who visits. During our time together you’ll immerse yourself in local Italian living. You will be introduced to my friends as we explore Naples and the pretty, picturesque villages and must see attractions that dot the beautiful stretches of Campania’s landscape. We will tour a beautiful winery to sip and savor some of Campania’s best wines and enjoy lunches and dinners at charming village trattorias and restaurants. We will seek out fresh, seasonal ingredients at the local markets, and then put them to good use in the aroma-filled kitchen of my home with some of my favorite Italian grandmothers and seasoned chefs. Departure after breakfast the following day with delicious memories of a lifetime!

Sicily

Your last 3 days will begin with guided sightseeing in Palermo’s ornate city of piazzas, markets, and churches. You’ll relish the baroque style of the city’s historic sites, and savor authentic Sicilian culinary specialties that have been handed down for generations. As you tour the perimeter of the beautiful island of Sicily, you’ll also enjoy a cruise to the small island of Motya for a museum tour, as well as escorted tours through Sicily’s centuries-old UNESCO World Heritage Sites such as the elaborate Villa Romana del Casale in Piazza Armerina. From palaces to churches, you’ll encounter ancient works of art, architecture, and archaeological treasures. You’ll travel to Syracuse – birthplace of mathematician Archimedes – for a guided sightseeing tour that includes the 5th-century-BC Greek theater. Your Sicily tour package concludes on the beautiful Taormina Riviera with an opportunity to visit Mount Etna – Europe’s largest active volcano. A sumptuous meal will put the finishing touch on your beautiful Italy tour of the ancient island of Sicily – with new memories to cherish for years to come.
